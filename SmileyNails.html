About Us

At Smiley Nails, we designed our shop to offer a relaxing ambiance and soothing sounds, creating the most comfortable and enjoyable atmosphere. We provide the highest quality nail services, including manicures, pedicures, acrylics, dipping powder, and more, all in a very clean environment with state-of-the-art ventilation so you can relax and enjoy your experience.

Our tools are always sterilized and sanitized for your safety. We care about our clients and take pride in treating you like royalty. Give us a try, and you will see the difference. We are the best in the Meridian and Eagle, Idaho area.

Host a party for weddings, birthdays, proms, bachelorette parties, sweet 16s, ladies' day/night out, or any special occasion. Just give us a call to make the arrangements. Ask about our group discounts.
